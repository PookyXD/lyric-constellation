01

the idea

music carries emotion in ways words alone cannot describe. every lyric line holds a feeling — joy, sadness, anger, fear. lyric constellation makes that invisible emotional journey visible.

02

how it works

lyrics are fetched automatically from Genius. every line runs through a transformer emotion model — the same architecture powering modern AI. each line becomes a star, sized by emotional intensity, connected across the song's journey.

03

the tech

built entirely in Python — FastAPI, Matplotlib, Pillow, and the Hartmann emotion transformer model. the frontend is pure HTML, CSS and JavaScript. no frameworks. no shortcuts.
